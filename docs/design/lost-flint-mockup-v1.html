Hubbard & Co. Drug Store
1898 – 1971
Two-story commercial block · W. 2nd St. · Carriage Town
Built in 1898 by John Hubbard, the building anchored the corner of W. 2nd and Detroit Street through the auto-industry boom. Razed in 1971 during the I-475 corridor clearance. The pressed-tin cornice and Renaissance Revival storefront were rare survivals of the pre-Buick commercial landscape.
